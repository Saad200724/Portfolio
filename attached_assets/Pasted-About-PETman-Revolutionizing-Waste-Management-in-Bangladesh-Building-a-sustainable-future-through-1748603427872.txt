Who We Are
PETman collects and responsibly recycles household and industrial waste — including PET plastic bottles, paper, fabric, and metal — and connects communities, offices, and individuals to waste collectors to build a transparent and sustainable circular economy in Bangladesh.

Our Mission
To revolutionize waste management in Bangladesh through a tech-driven platform that enables smart collection, community incentives, and partnerships — empowering households and corporates to recycle plastic, paper, fabric, and metal.

Our Vision
To create a clean, user-friendly, and sustainable society by transforming how Bangladesh manages and recycles reusable waste.
